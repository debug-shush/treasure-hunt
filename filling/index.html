How do I input a puzzle?
Input your grid size, and hit the "Display Grid" button. After that, click on the cells and type in the numbers. Once you think you've got it right, hit the "Solve" button.
You can also input a "puzzle string", which is something like "3x3:2a3a3a2aa"
